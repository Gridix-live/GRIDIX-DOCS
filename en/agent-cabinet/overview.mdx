The Agent Cabinet is a dedicated app for agents and brokers working with developers on Gridix. It provides everything you need to browse projects, track leads, monitor earnings, and manage your profile.

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Agent Cabinet dashboard — KPI cards, revenue chart, and activity feed
</Note>

## Dashboard

Your home screen shows four key metrics:

- **Commission Paid** — total earnings already paid out
- **Commission Pending** — earnings awaiting payout
- **Leads** — total leads you've generated
- **Catalog** — number of available projects

Below the metrics, you'll find:
- **Revenue chart** — earnings trend over time
- **Activity feed** — recent actions and updates

## Analytics

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Agent Analytics — CRM view showing lead funnel and source breakdown
</Note>

Two analytics views are available:

### CRM View
- Total leads and conversion rate
- Stage funnel — leads broken down by sales pipeline stage
- Source breakdown — where your leads come from

### Finance View
- Total paid and pending amounts
- Number of payouts
- Payout registry — detailed list of all payouts with status, date, and amount

Switch between views using the toolbar. Filter by period: 7, 30, 90 days, or full year.

## Contacts

View all the contacts you've generated:

- Searchable list of all your leads
- Click any contact to see details in a side panel:
  - Name, email, phone
  - Number of leads from this person
  - Last interaction date
  - Associated projects

## Catalog

Browse all projects available in your developer's portfolio:

- **Grid view** or **Map view** toggle
- Search and filter projects
- View project details in a drawer
- Share project links with potential buyers
- Open public project pages

<Note>
  **Video:** Browsing the project catalog and sharing a project link with a buyer
</Note>

## Settings

Manage your agent profile across four sections:

- **Company** — personal or company info (name, phone, tax ID, legal address, bank details, IBAN, billing currency, VAT status)
- **Account** — password and security settings
- **Notifications** — email notification preferences
- **Data** — contracts, digital signatures, and profile data management

## Partner Program

Access the embedded partner program interface to view your referral status, generate links, and track referral performance.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive invitation">
    A developer invites you to join their agent network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Fill in your company information, bank details, and sign the agreement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse projects">
    Explore available projects in the Catalog tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start selling">
    Share project links with potential buyers and track leads in your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
