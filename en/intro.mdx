## Quick start for developers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at [gridix.app](https://gridix.app). New accounts get a 14-day free trial with full Pro access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first project">
    Import data from Excel/Google Sheets or set up a project manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload floor plans">
    Upload floor plan images and mark apartments on each floor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Embed on your website">
    Generate widget code and paste it into your site. Your floor plan is live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
