The Analytics dashboard gives you a complete picture of how your projects are performing. Track views, leads, conversion rates, and identify your top-performing projects and apartments.

<Note>
Analytics is available on the Pro plan. Access it from the sidebar in the admin dashboard.
</Note>

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Analytics dashboard — full view with KPI cards and charts
</Note>

## Filters

Customize your view with:

- **Date range** — quick presets: 7, 30, 90 days, or all time
- **Custom dates** — pick specific start and end dates
- **Project filter** — view analytics for a specific project or all projects

## Key Metrics

Four KPI cards are displayed at the top:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Total Views | Number of floor plan views across all projects |
| Total Leads | Number of leads submitted |
| Conversion Rate | Percentage of views that resulted in a lead |
| Total Apartments | Number of apartments across your projects |

## Charts

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Analytics charts — Views over time and Leads over time line charts
</Note>

### Views Over Time
Line chart showing daily view counts for the selected period. Helps identify traffic trends and peaks.

### Leads Over Time
Line chart showing daily lead submissions. Compare with views to understand conversion patterns.

### Top Projects
Grouped bar chart comparing views and leads across your best-performing projects. Quickly see which projects attract the most attention and generate the most leads.

## Top Apartments

Cards showing your most-viewed apartments with:
- Apartment number
- Project name
- View count

## Apartment Stats

Pie chart showing the distribution of apartment statuses:
- Available
- Sold
- Reserved

## Projects Detail Table

Table listing your projects with conversion metrics. Compare performance across projects and identify where to focus your efforts.
