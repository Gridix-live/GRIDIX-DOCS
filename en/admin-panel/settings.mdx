The Settings page lets you manage your company information, account security, notifications, and data. Access it from the sidebar in the admin dashboard.

## Company Profile

### Company Information

Configure your company details used in invoices, partner program, and communications:

- **Company name**
- **Email address**
- **Phone number**
- **Physical address**
- **Tax ID / VAT number**
- **Bank details** (bank name, IBAN)
- **Billing currency**
- **VAT payer status**

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Company settings form — all fields visible
</Note>

### Currency Settings

Set your default currency for project pricing, invoices, and partner payouts. Supported currencies include USD, EUR, GEL, and others.

## Account Security

Manage your personal account:

- **Password** — update your login password
- **Language preference** — set default interface language
- **Session management** — active sessions and logout

<Tip>
Use a strong, unique password and don't share login credentials. Log out when using shared devices.
</Tip>

## Notifications

Configure how you receive alerts about new leads, tasks, and system updates.

**Channels:**
- Email notifications
- Push notifications (browser)
- Telegram notifications (requires username verification)

**Events:**
- New lead created
- Task due reminder
- Payment received
- System updates

See [Notifications](/en/notifications/overview) for detailed configuration.

## Manager Accounts

Add and manage team members from the Manager Accounts section within Settings. See [Managing Team Members](/en/admin-panel/managers) for details.

## Data & Export

Export your data for backup or analysis:

- **Projects** — export as JSON or CSV
- **Leads** — export with filters applied
- **Invoices** — download as PDF
- **Contracts** — manage digital signatures and agreements

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Changes not saving">
    Ensure you clicked "Save". Check for validation errors (red highlights). Refresh the page and try again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't access certain settings">
    Verify your role — managers have limited settings access. Only developers can modify company settings, subscriptions, and team members.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
