Gridix offers two plans: Basic and Pro. Both support unlimited projects.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Basic | Pro |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Unlimited projects | Yes | Yes |
| Floor plan visualization | Yes | Yes |
| Lead collection | Yes | Yes |
| Custom fields | Yes | Yes |
| CRM integration (AmoCRM, Bitrix24) | No | Yes |
| Custom domains | No | Yes |
| Agent Network | No | Yes |
| Advanced analytics | No | Yes |
| Priority support | No | Yes |

<Note>
For pricing details, [contact our sales team](https://t.me/gridix_bot) or visit [gridix.app](https://gridix.app).
</Note>

## Free Trial

New accounts get a **14-day free trial** of the Pro plan:

- Full access to all Pro features
- No credit card required
- Automatically converts to Basic after trial ends

## Subscription Discounts

Save more with longer commitments:

| Duration | Discount |
|----------|----------|

| 6 months | 10% |
| 1 year | 20% |
| 2 years | 30% |
| 3 years | 50% |

## When to Choose Basic

- Getting started with Gridix
- Basic floor plan and lead collection needs
- No CRM integration required

## When to Choose Pro

- Need CRM integration (AmoCRM or Bitrix24)
- Custom domain for project pages
- Agent network and partner management
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Priority support
