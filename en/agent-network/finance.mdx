Track and manage commissions for your partner agents. Each partner has their own financial breakdown visible in the Agent Network drawer.

## Commission Tracking

Each partner's Finance tab shows:

- **Commission rate** — the current percentage (editable in Settings tab)
- **Pending commission** — amounts earned but not yet paid
- **Paid commission** — total amounts already paid out
- **Payout history** — detailed list of all payouts

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Payout confirmation dialog — marking a pending payout as paid
</Note>

## Payout History

The payout list shows each payment with:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Amount | Payment amount |
| Date | When the payout was recorded |
| Lead | Associated lead name (if applicable) |
| Status | Paid or pending |

## Marking Payouts as Paid

When you've transferred funds to a partner:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open partner drawer">
    Click the partner in the Agent Network list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Finance tab">
    Switch to the Finance tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select pending payouts">
    Choose which pending payouts to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as paid">
    Click "Mark as Paid" and confirm. The payouts move to paid status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adjusting Commission Rates

To change a partner's commission rate:

1. Open the partner drawer
2. Go to the **Settings** tab
3. Edit the commission rate field
4. Save changes

The new rate applies to future leads only. Existing pending commissions are not recalculated.

## Overview Stats

The Agent Network overview page shows aggregate financial metrics:

- **Total sales volume** — revenue generated through all agents
- **Total pending commissions** — sum of unpaid amounts across all partners

These numbers update in real time as leads and payouts are processed.
