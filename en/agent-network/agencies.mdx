Click on any partner in the Agent Network list to open their detail drawer. The drawer has four tabs: Overview, Leads, Finance, and Settings.

## Overview Tab

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Partner drawer — Overview tab showing stats, contact info, and bank details
</Note>

The overview shows:

- **Partner info** — name, avatar/initials, type (Agency or Individual)
- **Status badge** — current status with color indicator
- **Contact details** — phone and email
- **Performance stats**:
  - Sales volume
  - Deals (closed / active)
  - Balance (pending commission)
- **Bank details** — bank name, IBAN, billing currency, VAT status

If the partner status is "Needs Correction", the rejection reason is displayed here.

## Leads Tab

View all leads attributed to this partner:

- Searchable list within the tab
- Each lead shows: name, project, apartment, status, date
- Click a lead to see full details

See [Agent Leads](/en/agent-network/leads) for more on how lead attribution works.

## Finance Tab

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Partner drawer — Finance tab showing pending and paid payouts
</Note>

Track commissions for this specific partner:

- **Commission rate** — current percentage
- **Pending payouts** — amounts not yet paid
- **Paid payouts** — completed payments with dates
- **Mark as paid** — confirm individual payouts

See [Agent Finance](/en/agent-network/finance) for detailed payout management.

## Settings Tab

Configure partner-specific options:

### Management
- Edit commission rate
- Change partner status (active, blocked, needs correction)
- Add rejection reason when returning an application

### Contracts
- View agreement signing status
- Upload or download contracts
- Track contract completion

### Signature
- Digital signature management
- Signature verification status

### Review
- View and edit original application details
- Review partner profile information
