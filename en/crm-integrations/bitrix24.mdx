title: "Bitrix24 Integration"
description: "Connect Bitrix24 to Gridix for automatic lead submission and deal status synchronization"
---

Connect Gridix with Bitrix24 so that leads from your floor plan widgets are automatically created as deals in Bitrix24, with deal stages synchronized with your Gridix project.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Bitrix24 account">
    If you don't have a Bitrix24 account yet, create one at bitrix24.com.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the Gridix app in Bitrix24">
    Open the Gridix application page in the Bitrix24 marketplace and install it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize and finish installation">
    After installation, sign in using your Bitrix24 account details and complete the setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works

- Gridix stores a single Bitrix24 connection per Gridix account.
- For each Gridix project, you select a Bitrix24 pipeline (deal category).
- After selecting a pipeline, Gridix creates a local funnel with all statuses and sets up stage mapping for synchronization.

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Bitrix24 integration settings — pipeline selection and stage mapping
</Note>

## Lead Flow

When a visitor submits an inquiry on your embedded floor plan:

1. A lead is created in Gridix with apartment and contact details
2. The lead is automatically sent to Bitrix24 as a new deal
3. The deal is placed in the configured pipeline
4. Stage changes in either system are synchronized

## Disconnecting

To remove the Bitrix24 integration, go to your project settings and disconnect the pipeline. This stops new leads from being sent but does not affect existing deals in Bitrix24.
