Gridix offers two partnership models with different commission structures and levels of involvement.

## Direct Management

Direct partners create and manage client accounts themselves. This model is for agencies, integrators, and freelancers who provide hands-on service.

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register as a partner">
    Sign up and get approved for direct management.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add clients">
    Create Gridix accounts for your clients and set up their projects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage accounts">
    Provide ongoing support: project setup, floor plans, CRM integration, customization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn commission">
    Receive commission from every client payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Commission Tiers

| Active Projects | Commission Rate |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| 0 – 9 | 40% |
| 10+ | 50% |

Commission is calculated from client subscription payments. As your portfolio grows past 10 projects, your rate increases to 50% on all clients.

### Best For

- Digital agencies
- CRM integrators
- Freelance consultants
- Anyone who can provide setup and support services

## Referral

Referral partners share a link and earn from signups — no technical involvement needed. Gridix uses a 2-level referral system.

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your referral link">
    Generate a personalized referral link in your partner dashboard. Add UTM parameters to track campaigns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the link">
    Share via social media, email, blog, or any channel. You can also generate a QR code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Client signs up">
    When someone registers through your link and subscribes, you earn commission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Level 2 earnings">
    If your referral becomes a partner and brings their own clients, you earn from their clients too.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Commission Levels

| Level | Rate | How It Works |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Level 1 | 20% | You referred the client directly |
| Level 2 | 10% | Your referral referred the client |

### Level 2 Example

1. **You** invite **Partner A** using your referral link
2. **Partner A** becomes a referral partner
3. **Partner A** invites **Client B**
4. **Partner A** earns **20%** from Client B's payments (Level 1)
5. **You** earn **10%** from Client B's payments (Level 2)

### Referral Analytics

Your dashboard tracks all referral link performance:

- **Click count** — how many people clicked your link
- **Registrations** — how many signed up
- **Traffic sources** — direct, YouTube, Telegram, Instagram, Facebook, blog, email
- **UTM tracking** — custom utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign parameters
- **Conversion funnel** — from click to registration to paid subscription

### Best For

- Marketers and bloggers
- Real estate consultants
- Anyone with contacts in the developer community
- Passive income seekers

## Comparison

| | Direct Management | Referral |
|---|---|---|
| Commission | 40–50% | 20% (L1) + 10% (L2) |
| Client management | Yes, you manage | No involvement |
| Technical setup | You handle it | Client self-serves |
| Multi-level earnings | No | Yes (2 levels) |
| Best for | Agencies, integrators | Marketers, consultants |
| Start time | After approval | Immediate |

## Earnings & Withdrawals

Both partner types can:

- View earnings in real time on the dashboard
- See income history charts and breakdowns
- Submit payout requests when ready
- Track payout status (pending, approved, paid)

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I do both direct management and referral?">
    Yes. You can manage some clients directly (40–50% commission) and refer others via link (20% commission). Your dashboard tracks both.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When can I withdraw earnings?">
    You can submit a payout request at any time from your Account section. Processing typically takes up to 7 days.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does Level 2 referral tracking work?">
    When someone registers through your link and later becomes a referral partner themselves, any clients they bring are tracked as your Level 2 referrals. You earn 10% from their payments automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What analytics do I get as a referral partner?">
    Full link analytics: clicks, registrations, traffic sources, UTM parameters, and conversion funnel data. Everything is visible in your partner dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
