The Gridix Partner Program lets you earn commissions in two ways: by referring clients through a link, or by directly managing their accounts. Choose the model that fits your business.

## Two Ways to Earn

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Direct Management" icon="users" href="/en/partners/types#direct-management">
    Add and manage clients yourself. Earn 40–50% commission depending on your client count.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referral" icon="link" href="/en/partners/types#referral">
    Share your referral link. Earn 20% from direct referrals and 10% from second-level referrals.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Commission Structure

### Direct Management

You create and manage client accounts directly:

| Clients (projects) | Commission |
|---------------------|------------|
| 0 – 9 | 40% |
| 10+ | 50% |

### Referral (2-Level System)

Share your referral link and earn from two levels:

| Level | Commission | Description |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| Level 1 | 20% | Client you referred directly |
| Level 2 | 10% | Client referred by your referral |

**How Level 2 works:** You invite Client A. Client A becomes a referral partner and invites Client B. You earn 10% from Client B's payments, and Client A earns 20%.

## Partner Dashboard

Your partner dashboard has 6 sections:

- **Overview** — earnings summary, income chart, traffic breakdown, commission rates
- **Referrals** — generate referral links with UTM parameters, QR codes
- **Clients** — list of all your clients with subscription status and projects
- **Instructions** — video tutorials and program guidelines
- **Account** — profile settings, withdrawal requests, payout history
- **Changelog** — platform updates and release notes

## Referral Analytics

Track the performance of your referral links in real time:

- **Clicks** — total link clicks
- **Registrations** — signups from your link
- **Traffic sources** — where visitors come from (direct, social, email, etc.)
- **UTM tags** — track campaigns with custom utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign
- **Conversion funnel** — from click to registration to paid subscription

## Earnings & Payouts

All earnings are tracked transparently in your dashboard:

- View total earned, available for withdrawal, and pending amounts
- Income history chart showing earnings over time
- Submit payout requests directly from your account
- Track payout status (pending, approved, paid)
