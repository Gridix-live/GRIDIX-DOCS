Configure how and when you receive notifications about important events. Access notification settings from Settings in the admin dashboard.

<Note>
  **Screenshot:** Notification settings panel — channels and event toggles
</Note>

## Channels

Gridix supports three notification channels:

### Email
Receive notifications to your account email address. Toggle on/off.

### Push
Browser push notifications for real-time alerts. Toggle on/off.

### Telegram
Receive notifications via Telegram. Requires setup:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your Telegram username">
    In the notification settings, enter your Telegram username.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Complete the verification process to link your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable">
    Toggle Telegram notifications on.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Events

Choose which events trigger notifications:

| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| New lead | A visitor submitted an inquiry through your widget |
| Task due | A scheduled task is due for action |
| Payment received | A subscription payment was processed |
| System update | Platform updates and announcements |

Each event can be toggled independently. Combine with any channel — for example, enable email for new leads and Telegram for payment notifications.
